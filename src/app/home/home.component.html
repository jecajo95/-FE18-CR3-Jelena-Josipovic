Our philosophy translates into a rediscovery of values ​​and flavors of the past. The roots represent our origins, our history, the foundations that have made us what we are today. Local traditions and the most innovative techniques coexist in our kitchen, there are no rules, there are no limits. Gambero is housed in a 1900 villa in the heart of Padua, home to the ancient and prestigious Ganzetti furrier's shop. In the dining room, comfortable fabric armchairs as if to recall the idea of ​​a living room at home, because this is how we want our guests to feel, protected by a welcoming, elegant but free from formality environment.

Why choose us

Roots hidden in the earth and roots on the plate, interpreted according to the season to accompany guests on this journey of rediscovery of matter, between past and present. A universe of scents, tastes, noises, sensations experienced as children and which are revived today in the memory and on the plate, in a whirlwind of flavors that color a course, an instant, a situation.
